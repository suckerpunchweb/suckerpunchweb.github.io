ABOUT

kotku is an indie rock/alternative/punk band fronted by coco jartos, a songwriter, visual artist, performer, and poet from atlanta. kotku is known for a performance art style onstage that relies heavily on the theatrics of costume, makeup, and audio/visual technology experimentation. coco has a background in theater, film, and ensemble stagework, as well as experience in writing, directing, editing, and solo performance.
